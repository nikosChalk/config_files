In order to sync settings download the "settings sync" plugin
https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=Shan.code-settings-sync

Follow the steps and use the Gist ID: 48b2d2509bb867a40e3c080a8983823b
Also toggle auto-download on startup
And  toggle auto-upload on change


TL;DR: 
1.) Shift + Alt + D
2.) Paste Gist ID: 48b2d2509bb867a40e3c080a8983823b
3.) Press Enter
4.) Ctrl + P ---> ">sync" ---> "Advanced" ---> toggle auto-download and auto-upload
